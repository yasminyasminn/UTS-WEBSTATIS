About Us

What is TUNIXPACE

TUNIXPACE is the epitome of simple and elegant style in the world of tunics. With our renowned reputation, we curate a collection of tunics that exude sophistication and timelessness. Each piece is meticulously crafted to embody the perfect balance of minimalism and grace, ensuring that you effortlessly radiate elegance in every step. We understand the importance of preserving the sanctity of the female aura, and each tunic is crafted to provide coverage without compromising on fashion. Explore our website and discover a harmonious blend of sophistication, elegance, and respect for the hijab, empowering women to express their personal style while upholding their values. Indulge in our exquisite selection of tunics and experience the beauty of fashion that embraces and celebrates the essence of modest femininity.

What is the vision of TUNIXPACE?

Menjadi pionir dalam mengarahkan tren fashion muslimah, mengusung gaya yang simple, elegan, dan casual.

Misi

What is the mission of TUNIXPACE?

1. Menyajikan produk fashion muslimah yang tinggi kualitasnya dan ramah lingkungan.

2. Menawarkan beragam koleksi fashion dengan pilihan warna yang memukau.

3. Menempatkan kepuasan pelanggan sebagai prioritas utama melalui pelayanan terbaik.

4. Membangun hubungan yang kuat dengan semua pihak terlibat dalam bisnis kami.

5. Mendukung penampilan muslimah sesuai standar aurat dengan desain yang longgar dan warna earth tone yang klasik.
